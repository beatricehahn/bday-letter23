"Dear Emily"

A letter to an amazing person!

It all seems to fly by quicker with each iteration, doesn’t it?
But the one great thing about time moving quickly is that I see and hear snapshots of your life and they are flipping by like a little animation presenting a story within a story.

I don’t know what it is about our friendship that works, as it seems like it is suspended in a bubble outside of regular time
because I never feel like I stray too far from being close at heart when I need saving.

As if our friendship is a lone boat out in the ocean far away
That appears within arm’s reach when I’m being washed out by the ocean.
And once we begin talking again it feels as if the boat has been right there always.

Or like a piece of wood that gets rained on so one would think it would be cold, damp, and difficult to spark,
But it’s core is always warm in the same way that you have always been kind,
genuine, and there for me when I felt cold, stiff, and alone.
Reaching out to you never felt awkward or difficult even after a large amount of chronological time had passed.
Even though I already valued our friendship deeply before, I’ve learned to cherish it more as I grew older.

I am thankful for your existence and my friendship with you.
I wish that 2024 unfolds with many happy memories that overpower any sadness.
It would be an honor to continue being a piece in your life and hear more about your dreams and career milestones.

I love you dearly,
And while memories pass and go my mind all the time,
Even if some become fuzzy,
Even if I may forget our exact conversations
And the exact experiences we had in person
I understand that my respect for you is etched well
And will endure all the storms that may blow.
